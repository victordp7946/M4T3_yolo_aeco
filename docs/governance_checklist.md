\# Governance Checklist (Safety PPE Dataset)



\## Privacy \& Ethics

\- \[ ] Images are used for \*\*academic/prototype\*\* purposes only.

\- \[ ] Avoid personally identifiable information (faces are incidental; no identity inference).

\- \[ ] Do not use for surveillance or disciplinary actions without policy/legal review.



\## Data Minimization

\- \[ ] Store only images necessary for training/evaluation.

\- \[ ] Avoid sensitive scenes unrelated to PPE detection.



\## Transparency

\- \[ ] Document model limitations (small/occluded PPE, low recall).

\- \[ ] Provide metrics + examples of failure cases.



\## Bias \& Representativeness

\- \[ ] Include varied worker appearances, PPE colors, lighting, and site types.

\- \[ ] Avoid overfitting to a single uniform/PPE style.



\## Safety \& Deployment Constraints

\- \[ ] Not reliable as a sole safety enforcement mechanism.

\- \[ ] Should be used as decision support with human verification.
